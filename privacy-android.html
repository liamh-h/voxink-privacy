VoxInk Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Overview

VoxInk is a voice input and translation app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Key principle: Your voice and text are processed in real-time and never stored. We do not maintain any database of user recordings or transcriptions.

Data We Collect

Voice Audio

  • Captured only when you press the record button.
  • Streamed in real-time to Soniox (speech-to-text service) for transcription.
  • Audio is never stored — processed in real-time and immediately discarded.

Transcribed Text

  • Sent to Google Gemini API for AI polishing or translation.
  • Text is never stored on our servers — processed and immediately discarded.
  • We do not maintain any database of user transcriptions.

Account Information

  • If you sign in with Google, we store a hashed version of your email as a user identifier.
  • Used solely for account identification and quota tracking.
  • We do not sell, share, or disclose your email to third parties.

Usage Data

  • We track aggregate usage (total seconds per mode) for quota management.
  • No personal content is included in usage data.

Accessibility Service

VoxInk uses Android's Accessibility Service to insert dictated text directly into text fields across apps. The service:

  • Only accesses the text field you are currently typing in
  • Does not read screen content, passwords, browsing history, or messages from other apps
  • Does not collect or transmit any data from other apps
  • Can be disabled at any time in Settings > Accessibility > VoxInk

Clipboard

VoxInk may temporarily place dictated text in the system clipboard to facilitate text insertion. This text is overwritten by your next clipboard action and is not stored by VoxInk.

Data Storage

All preferences are stored locally using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences. No user data is stored on remote servers beyond a hashed user ID and aggregate usage counts.

Children's Privacy

VoxInk is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

  • Delete account data by clearing app data in Android Settings
  • Disable all permissions (microphone, accessibility, overlay) at any time
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
