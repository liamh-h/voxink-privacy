VoxInk Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Overview

VoxInk ("we", "our", "the app") is a voice input and translation app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Voice Audio

  • Your voice is captured by the device microphone when you actively press the record button.
  • Audio is streamed in real-time to Soniox (speech-to-text service) for transcription.
  • Audio is never stored — it is processed in real-time and immediately discarded.
  • We do not record, save, or retain any audio files.

Transcribed Text

  • Your transcribed text is sent to Google Gemini API for AI polishing or translation.
  • Text is never stored on our servers — it is processed and immediately discarded.
  • We do not maintain any database of user transcriptions.

Account Information

  • If you sign in with Google, we store your email address (hashed) as a user identifier.
  • Your email is used solely for account identification and quota tracking.
  • We do not sell, share, or disclose your email to third parties.

Usage Data

  • We track aggregate usage statistics (total seconds of recording per mode) for quota management.
  • No personal content is included in usage data.

Accessibility Service

VoxInk uses Android's Accessibility Service to insert dictated text directly into text fields across apps. The Accessibility Service:

  • ONLY accesses the text field you are currently typing in
  • DOES NOT read your screen content, passwords, browsing history, or messages
  • DOES NOT collect or transmit any data from other apps
  • Can be disabled at any time in Settings → Accessibility → VoxInk

Clipboard

VoxInk may temporarily place your dictated text in the system clipboard to facilitate text insertion. This text is overwritten by your next clipboard action and is not stored by VoxInk.

Data Storage

  • All app preferences are stored locally on your device using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences.
  • No user data is stored on remote servers beyond hashed user ID and aggregate usage counts.

Children's Privacy

VoxInk is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

  • You can delete your account data by clearing the app's data in Android Settings.
  • You can disable all permissions (microphone, accessibility, overlay) at any time.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove all local data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on this page.
