Delete Your Account

VoxInk uses Google Sign-In and does not store any personal data on our servers. Your voice and text are processed in real-time and immediately discarded. Deleting your account means revoking VoxInk's access to your Google account.

How to delete your account

1

Revoke Google access

Go to Google Account → Security → Third-party apps with account access. Find VoxInk and click Remove Access.

2

Uninstall the app

Uninstall VoxInk from your device. This removes all local data including cached preferences and login state.

3

Done

Your account is fully deleted. Since we don't store any personal data, voice recordings, or text on our servers, there is nothing left to remove.

Manage Google Account Permissions →

What data does VoxInk store?

VoxInk stores minimal data for service operation:

  • On your device: Login state, app preferences (encrypted)
  • On our server: Hashed user ID + aggregate usage seconds (no personal data)
  • Never stored: Voice audio, transcribed text, messages, or any content

Uninstalling the app removes all on-device data. The server-side hashed ID and usage counter cannot be linked back to your identity.
